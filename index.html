Tanner Park

Tanner Park trail is about a 1 mile round trip walk. It is an off leash dog area that usually has quite a lot of traffic. If your dogs enjoy meeting and playing with others this is the place for you! It is a very easy walk with 2 different places for your dogs to get wet.

Trail Details
Distance: 1.0 miles
Time: .5 hours at a leisurely pace
Difficulty: Easy
